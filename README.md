# 🚀 NASA Space Apps Challenge 2025: 
# Interactive Digital Comic
 
> **Note:** This repository is a personal fork of the original project developed during the NASA International Space Apps Challenge 2025.

## 🌌 About the Project
During the hackathon, I was part of a multidisciplinary team with the goal of creating an **interactive and gamified comic book** aimed at teaching science, astronomy, and solar cycles to children. 

By combining an engaging narrative with interactivity, our mission was to make learning complex scientific concepts more accessible, playful, and fun. Throughout the challenge, we collaborated on developing the plot, characters, visual identity, and the project's digital platform.

## 🌍 The Hackathon Experience
The **NASA International Space Apps Challenge 2025** is one of the largest collaborative hackathons in the world, hosted by NASA in partnership with several international space agencies. 

The project was built from scratch in an environment of intense collaboration, with support and mentorship from professionals in the technology, science, and education sectors. The hackathon dynamic required strong time management, adaptability, and the ability to work under pressure, alongside a constant exchange of ideas among people from different backgrounds and countries.

## 👨‍💻 My Role & Contributions
As a member of this team, I actively participated in multiple stages of the project, applying skills in creativity, communication, planning, and problem-solving. My main contributions included:

* **Conceptualization & Storytelling:** Collaborated on developing the core narrative, characters, and educational flow.
* **Design & Visual Identity:** Helped shape the engaging and kid-friendly aesthetics of the comic.

## 💡 Key Takeaways
This experience allowed me to improve my ability to think creatively and interdisciplinarily, transforming complex scientific concepts into educational experiences. 

It also reinforced my interest in projects that bridge **technology, art, and social impact**, showcasing how innovation can be used to inspire new generations to engage with science and space exploration. ✨
